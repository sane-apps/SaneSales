SaneSales vs Spreadsheets

Spreadsheets are flexible, but they make you do the work. Every morning you still have to pull numbers from LemonSqueezy, Gumroad, and Stripe, paste them somewhere, and hope yesterday's formula still points at the right cells.

Who Should Use What

If you love building your own dashboards and don't mind maintaining them, a spreadsheet is still fine. If you want to check today's sales fast, then unlock trends and history only when they become useful, SaneSales is the simpler system.

Start with the free tier. If you hit the point where you want to compare today vs yesterday, review the last 7 days, or export real order history, that is the moment Pro earns its keep.
