How It Works

SaneSales syncs your sales data from iPhone to Apple Watch automatically. The Watch app reads cached order data from the shared app group — no separate API key setup needed. Connect your providers on iPhone or Mac, and your Watch has the data.

Watch Face Widgets

Beyond the full Watch app, you can add SaneSales complications to your watch face. The inline complication shows today's revenue in a single line of text. The circular complication shows your revenue and order count. The rectangular complication gives you a mini dashboard with revenue and orders. All update automatically throughout the day.
