How to Track LemonSqueezy Sales on Mac & iPhone

LemonSqueezy is great for selling software licenses and digital products. But their dashboard is a browser tab you have to remember to check. Here's how to bring your LemonSqueezy sales into a native app on your device.

The Spreadsheet Way

  • 1
    Log into app.lemonsqueezy.com every morning
  • 2
    Navigate to Orders, filter by date range
  • 3
    Export CSV, open in Excel, subtract refunds manually
  • 4
    Repeat for every product. Copy into your master spreadsheet
  • 5
    Realize you forgot yesterday. Your spreadsheet is already stale

The SaneSales Way

  • ✓
    Settings → LemonSqueezy → Paste API Key. That's it.
  • ✓
    SaneSales fetches all your orders — even if you have thousands
  • ✓
    Refunds deducted automatically — you see net revenue, not overstated numbers
  • ✓
    Product breakdown — donut chart shows revenue per product, tap to explore
  • ✓
    Your API key stays in the device Keychain — hardware-encrypted, never transmitted

Where to Find Your LemonSqueezy API Key

Go to app.lemonsqueezy.com → Settings → API. Click "Create API Key", give it a name (like "SaneSales"), and copy the key. Paste it into SaneSales Settings. The key only needs read access — SaneSales never modifies your store.
